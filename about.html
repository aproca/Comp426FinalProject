Our Story


ViBE was created by four UNC Chapel Hill Students


On a stormy November night, four laptop screens shined brighter then the rest. While the rest of the UNC student was concering themselves with dreams of Thanksgiving break, these brave four students thought of a local music web application that would be the best program to ever be programmed by a team of programmers. The idea itself was come up with Everett, who after looking down at his lap in despair, lifted his eyes and uttered one word "Eureka."
